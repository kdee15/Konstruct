While Konstruct strives to stay out of your way as much as possible, there are still quite a few cool things we provide you with. Come inside and check them out!

Take Note:

We take great care to write great, useable documentation. So it might still be incomplete in places. Why don't you help out?

A note on best practises

Although Konstruct is developed to be as unobtrusive and simple as possible, there are a few code practises & patterns included. These are things we use daily, and I felt it was a good enough way to do things for core. Of course you are welcome to change that, and even make a pull request to get your way in.
