Build great things.

Konstruct is a toolbox, filled with tools you need to build great things with.

Konstruct is not a design application, therefore it won't give you pretty websites. That you need to do yourself, which is at it should be. Konstruct gives you a rock solid foundation & and the bricks you need to build your awesome sites on.

Konstruct gives you a toolbox of useful patterns and best practices, while leaving the site you are building completely in your hands.
